Franzy-OS

A personal operating system for running daily execution, weekly review, and long-term portfolio growth without depending on memory or scattered tools.

Live workflow Cowork + Obsidian Google Tasks + Calendar

What it is

A workflow, not just a dashboard

This system ties together planning, capture, review, and portfolio upkeep. The goal is simple: reduce mental overhead, protect deep work, and make useful achievements easier to surface and reuse later.

Operating rules
Today list max 5. Anything beyond that stays in inbox or moves to a later day.
One capture path. Ideas go into Google Tasks inbox or `scratchpad.md`, not memory.
Waiting items expire. Stale blockers are escalated or closed instead of lingering.
Deep work stays protected. Calendar reflects actual capacity, not wishful planning.
Every meaningful engineering decision can become evidence. The workflow helps surface that early.
